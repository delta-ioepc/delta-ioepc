DESIGN-A-THON “सुधारिएको गोठ”

"Design-A-Thon" is one of the main events of the national-level technical festival hosted by DELTA 3.0 of Purwanchal Engineering Campus, Dharan. The theme of this national event is “सुधारिएको गोठ” Cattle Farm Design Competition. Open to all-year students of BE, B.Arch., B.Sc. Ag, BVSc & A.H. from all over Nepal, this event aims to find innovative designs and promote sustainability. The registration fee is only Rs. 100 for individuals and only Rs. 200 per team with the registration deadline of the 20th of Poush (Midnight) and Submission deadline of the 30th of Poush (Midnight). All participant will be live demonstrating their designs on DELTA 3.0 5,6 and 7th of Magh. Registration link:https://forms.gle/wvKeH1fXo1dqQhBfA

MAKE-A-THON

The Make-A-Thon is an exciting event hosted by DELTA 3.0 where participants are required to make a bridge out of popsicles. Make-A-Thon aims to bring together students from around the nation to design and build innovative bridges. The bridges must be functional and showcase incredible strength and versatility.
